Hello! I am a French-American master's student doing an MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Oxford since September 2021. Before this, I was an undergraduate Honours Computer Science student at McGill University where I graduated with distinction in 2021. 

I have recently been doing research at McGill/Mila in the field of graph neural networks with Professor William Hamilton after receiving a McGill Science Undergraduate Research Award. The work explores the use of ego-nets to improve the capabilities and power of GNNs. We submitted a paper (of which I am the first author) on this work that was accepted to a special session of IEEE-ICASSP 2021. That paper can be viewed here. My profile with Mila can be found here.

I am the CTO and co-founder of Piriko, a real-time event planning app that allows users to follow and engage with events happening live in the present. Our amazing 6 person team has already launched closed alpha versions of the app on the iOS Store and the Google Play Store. We have recently entered into a contract with Concordia University. The school published an article about the startup that can be viewed here. 

I have a lot of international professional experience having been raised in Shanghai and Singapore in a bi-lingual home (English and French) before doing my undergraduate degree in Montreal.
